Vijay's

The Prestige

Located in the calm and central heart of Solapur.

A signature residential development.

Vijay’s The Prestige reflects our forward-looking approach to refined family living — where proportion, structure, and lifestyle amenities align.

This development is being undertaken through Gavasane and Khed Builders.

Amenities for calm, elevated living.

The Prestige is imagined as a quiet vertical neighbourhood — generous residences, considered amenity spaces, and circulation that feels intuitive rather than crowded.

Residence mix

14 units of 3BHK and 14 units of 4BHK, planned for light, cross-ventilation, and long-term family living.

Movement & parking

Basement + stilt parking with organised circulation and two lifts, so arrivals remain efficient and composed.

Kids play & everyday leisure

A dedicated kids play area and open pockets designed for everyday play, not just occasional use.

Rooftop rituals

A yoga hall and machan-style seating, curated for quiet gatherings, sunrise routines, and small celebrations.

Considered sustainability

Solar-powered common electricity supports lower maintenance over time while keeping shared spaces warmly lit.

Every element is designed to feel composed in daily use: from arrival and parking to lift lobbies, play areas, and rooftop rituals.
