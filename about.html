Company Story

Founded on technical confidence

Gavasane Builders was established in 2016 by three civil engineers who came together with a shared confidence in planning, construction, and execution.

This was not an inherited business, but a deliberately built company shaped by technical knowledge, on-site understanding, and a practical approach to development.

Every project is approached with the care of planning one’s own home — with close attention to layout, structural clarity, quality, and long-term livability.

Today, the company continues to build across residential and mixed-use developments in Solapur, with a growing record defined by timely completion, dependable quality, and grounded decision-making.

Technically grounded. Locally rooted. Carefully executed.
